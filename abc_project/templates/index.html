Bahrain - Saudi Transfer

Travel to any point of destination from Bahrain to KSA, or from KSA to Bahrain. Whether for individuals or groups, whether for business, family or leisure trips, Motornet can take you from Bahrain to various locations in Saudi Arabia such as Riyadh, Jubail, Al Ahsa, Taif, Jeddah, Dammam or Khobar; or from Saudi Arabia to any point in Bahrain from Muharraq to Manama to Zallaq. Plan for a customized one-day or several days itinerary, choose your destinations, set the date and leave the rest to Motornet!

Airport Transfer

Avail of hassle-free transport services to and from airports in Bahrain and KSA. Be transferred with utmost comfort and safety by professional drivers, and choose from a fleet of luxury limousines, SUVs & sedans. Get door to door service, meet and greet, flight monitoring, plus free cancellation.
